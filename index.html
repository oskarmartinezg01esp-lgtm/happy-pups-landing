Our Story

Where Every Dog is
Treated Like Royalty

At Happy Pups Pet Salon, we believe your dog deserves nothing but the very best. Our passionate team of certified groomers brings years of hands-on experience and a genuine, deep love for animals to every single appointment.

Located in the heart of Aventura, FL, we specialize in bringing out the absolute best in every pup — from tiny Yorkies to large Golden Retrievers — using only premium, pet-safe products and the gentlest techniques available.
